# Pattern: Incident Accountability

## Goal
Ensure incident response is owned before launch, not invented during crisis.

## Required pre-release definitions
- who receives the first alert
- who can disable the system
- who informs stakeholders
- who owns remediation
- who signs off on re-enablement

## Review questions
- Can the system be safely paused?
- Who has the authority to do that?
- Is the owner reachable at all required times?
- Are actions and decisions logged?
